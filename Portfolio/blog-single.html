HOW IT ALL STARTED

  • by: Adamu Aliyu
  • date: Jan 17, 2024
  • 4 Comments

Sometimes, when the world seem like hell, even though nothing is still changed... I still think of the world as hell. So, whenever i feel like dying and, & getting over it already, the only thing that makes me feel back alive is if i turn on my computer fron sleep and opened my google doc to start typing the shits i'm going through... I start typing and i never raise my finger on the keyboard until tha feeling of wanting to leave all the people that love me in this world finally goes away.

I didn't realize what i was doing at first... Only that i knew somehow that typing what i'm going through at some point if my life makes me... It makes me feel like a heavy weight has been lifted off my chase. And the thing is, if you'll ever ask me how i do it, i don't think i'll ever have an answer to that. Because, it was not some sort of a plan for me to someday publish a book or anything like that.

If you are still looking for that one person who will change your life, take a look in the mirror.

When i was writting my pages, it was like... It was more like me writting it just to lift the weight that i feel off my body. Because, Life...
Life is just.. "Life isn't fair" okay?
And i know some people might disagree with me on this but yeah. Life is indeed not fair sometimes, or perhaps, all the time.
Yeah and if you really think i'm wrong and that life is fair then, you really should consider looking around and having just even a peak at some other people's life and maybe, just maybe. Then you're likely to understand what i'm saying.
So, the truth is, i never actually turned back around to read any of those pages that i was writting when i was writting them. I just, i just kept going.. At first when i started, i thought of it as a very dumb idea because a friend of mine who's so much into literature got me into it.
She told me that she does same thing over and over whenver she feels angry, sad or even happy. And yeah, so i started. From writting a page a day to three pages, and then to 10 pages to even typing 20 pages in a day.
It just changes my entire mood whenever i start typing. To a point in my life where i don't even wanna stop writting anymore. I just write whatever that comes into my head, be it my story or just a scenerio that i want to happen to me. Yeah, just like.. My imaginations along side the reality that i face, to even the people that i meet on a daily basis.
